Welcome to Focus:

A tool to help you concentrate on what's important.

How It Works

When this extension is activated, you'll be unable to access any content on the main page of YouTube. While you can search for specific videos in the search bar, you won't see any of the recommended videos on the main page.

How It Helps You

When you're trying to concentrate, it's easy to jump to YouTube and drop half an hour on the first few videos that pop up.

Focus helps you spend more time working on what matters. By delivering a gentle reminder whenever you visit YouTube, Focus is a non-intrusive enhancement to your workflow.
