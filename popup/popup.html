Welcome to Focus:

A tool to help you concentrate on what's important.

How It Works

When this extension is activated, you'll be unable to access any content on the main pages of YouTube, Facebook, or Reddit.

How It Helps You

When you're trying to concentrate, it's easy to jump to YouTube, Facebook, or Reddit and start mindlessly scrolling through the content these sites give you.

Focus helps you spend more time working on what matters. By delivering a gentle reminder whenever you visit your favorite distracting site, Focus provides a non-intrusive and motivational enhancement to your workflow.
